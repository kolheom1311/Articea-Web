    For Customers

    1) Explore and Discover:

    - Browse through our extensive collection of handmade products. Use filters to narrow down your search by category, price, location, and artist. Each item comes with a detailed description and images to help you make an informed decision.


    2) Support Artists:

    - Read about the artists behind the creations. Learn about their stories, inspirations, and techniques. By purchasing their products, you directly support their craft and help them continue doing what they love.

    3) Purchase and Checkout:

    - Add your favorite items to the cart and proceed to checkout. Articea offers secure payment methods to ensure your transaction is safe and seamless. You can also choose from various shipping options to suit your needs.


    4) Track Your Order:

    - Once your order is placed, you’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details. Monitor the progress of your shipment and know exactly when to expect your unique, handmade item.


    5) Enjoy and Review:

    - Receive your product and enjoy the craftsmanship and uniqueness of your purchase. Leave a review for the artist to share your experience and help others discover their amazing work.

    For Artists

    1) Sign Up and Create Your Profile:

    - Begin your journey by signing up on Articea. Create a profile that showcases who you are, your background, and your artistic journey. Add a profile picture and a compelling bio to attract potential customers.


    2) List Your Products:

    - Upload high-quality images and descriptions of your handmade products. Provide details such as materials used, dimensions, and the story behind each piece. Set your prices and shipping options.

    3) Manage Your Store:

    - Use our intuitive dashboard to manage your listings, track orders, and handle customer inquiries. Update your product inventory as needed and keep your profile fresh with new creations.


    4) Receive Orders:

    - When a customer purchases one of your products, you’ll receive an order notification. Prepare the item for shipping and ensure it reaches the customer in perfect condition.


    5) Get Paid:

    - Once the customer confirms receipt of the item, your earnings will be transferred to your account. Articea offers secure payment options to ensure you get paid promptly and safely.

    Community Engagement

    1) Join Events and Workshops:

    - Participate in our virtual events and workshops to learn new skills, interact with artists, and become part of the Articea community. These events are designed to foster connections and inspire creativity.


    2) Stay Updated:

    - Follow our blog and social media channels for the latest news, artist spotlights, and upcoming events. Subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive offers and updates directly to your inbox.
